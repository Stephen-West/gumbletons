1. Introduction

This is volume {{compendium.volume}} of a set of volumes containing details of the Gumbleton one-name genealogical study carried out by Stephen West. When I began researching the Gumbleton family, most records were kept in handwritten notebooks. Soon after, I began copying some of the records into a computer database and, with the advent of the World Wide Web, making these available from a webserver. In the last ten years, my use of notebooks has declined and nearly all the Gumbleton one-name records are now contained in a computer database from which the online gumbleton.com version is generated. I am conscious that, for some years, there has been no permanent paper record of my Gumbleton research. These books are an attempt to address that problem and make the same information available in book form.

Please note that this is not intended to be a narrative book that can be read from cover to cover. Rather, it is a compendium of records of the Gumbleton family, collected from a wide range of sources and associated, wherever possible, with particular individuals. Wherever such individuals are identified, they have been given a 'code' consisting of a letter and four numbers (eg N0004). These codes can be cross-referenced in a separate volume which list all the individuals.
