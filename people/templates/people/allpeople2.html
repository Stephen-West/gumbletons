Gumbleton One Name Study. Volume 4: People


Introduction

This is volume 4 of a set of volumes containing details of the Gumbleton one-name genealogical study carried out by Stephen West. Nearly all the Gumbleton one-name records are contained in a computer database from which the online gumbleton.com version is generated. These books are an attempt to make the same information available in paper form.

Please note that this is not intended to be a narrative book that can be read from cover to cover. Rather, it is a compendium of records of the Gumbleton family, collected from a wide range of sources and associated, wherever possible, with particular individuals. Wherever such individuals are identified, they have been given a 'code' consisting of a letter and four numbers (eg N0004).

The present volume lists all the people in the database. The ordering is by their code numbers, so person P0001 should come before P0002 and both will be before Q0001. There is also an alphabetical list of people, sorted by surname and forenames, at the end of this volume.
